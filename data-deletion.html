Account and Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your nook account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to request account deletion

To request the deletion of your account, send us an email directly.

  1. Send an email to lucerocris22@gmail.com from the email address registered to your nook account.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request - nook
  3. In the body of the email, simply state that you want your account and data deleted.

What data is deleted?

Upon receiving your request, we will completely erase the following data from our database within 14 days:

  • Your account credentials (email and password/authentication links)
  • Your profile information
  • Any personal preferences, saved cafes, or user-generated activity tied to your account

Data retention

Once the deletion process is complete, your data cannot be recovered. We do not retain any of your personal information after the 14-day deletion window.
